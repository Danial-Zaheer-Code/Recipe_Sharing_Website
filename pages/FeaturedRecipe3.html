Lobster Thermidor

Ingredients:

  • 2 live lobsters (about 1.5 lbs each)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Gruyère cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Lobster:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add lobsters and cook for 8-10 minutes. Remove the lobsters and let them cool. Once cool, remove the meat from the shells and chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Make the Sauce:
    • In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add white wine and cook until reduced by half. Stir in heavy cream, Gruyère cheese, Dijon mustard, and chopped parsley. Cook until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Combine Lobster and Sauce:
    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Gently fold the lobster meat into the sauce until well combined.
  4. Prepare for Baking:
    • Place the lobster mixture back into the lobster shells. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and additional Gruyère cheese if desired.
  5. Bake and Serve:
    • Place the stuffed lobster shells on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the filling is hot. Serve immediately.
