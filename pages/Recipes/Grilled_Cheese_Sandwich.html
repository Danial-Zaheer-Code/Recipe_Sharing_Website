Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Instructions:

  1. Butter the Bread:
    • Spread butter evenly on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich:
    • Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in a skillet over medium heat. Add the cheese slices on top, then cover with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
  3. Grill the Sandwich:
    • Cook the sandwich for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted. Serve hot.
