Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs (basil, oregano, thyme)
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prep the chicken:
    • Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  2. Cook the chicken:
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Make the sauce:
    • In the same skillet, add minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    • Pour in the chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the skillet to deglaze, releasing any browned bits.
    • Stir in the heavy cream and bring the mixture to a simmer.
    • Add the Parmesan cheese and Italian herbs, stirring until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Combine Chicken and Sauce:
    • Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet and coat them with the sauce. Let them simmer in the sauce for an additional 5 minutes to soak up the flavors.
  5. Garnish and Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
    • Serve the creamy garlic Parmesan chicken over pasta, rice, or with a side of vegetables.
